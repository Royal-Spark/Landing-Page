Funding Glossary

RDT&E: Research, Development, Test, and Evaluation. Funds for developing new technologies and systems.

Procurement: Funding for purchasing and deploying a complete, mature system.

O&M: Operations and Maintenance. Funds for sustaining existing systems and operations.

MILPERS: Military Personnel. Funds for military pay, allowances, and travel.

MILCON: Military Construction. Funds for construction of military infrastructure.

Revolving Funds: Finance cyclic business-type operations without repeated congressional approval.
